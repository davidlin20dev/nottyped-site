Community Guidelines

Effective date: June 20, 2026

NotTyped is a place to share your handwriting with other people. Be respectful. Be human. These guidelines keep it that way for everyone.

What's not allowed

To keep NotTyped welcoming, the following are not permitted:

  • Hate speech
  • Harassment, bullying, or threats
  • Spam
  • Promotion of self-harm or suicide
  • Sexually explicit content
  • Impersonation of any person or entity
  • Handle squatting, or buying or selling handles
  • Doxxing or sharing others' private information
  • Copyright violation
  • Illegal activity

Consequences

When something crosses the line, we respond in proportion to the violation. Depending on severity and history, that can mean:

  1. Content removal — the offending note or comment is taken down.
  2. Warning — we let you know what happened and why.
  3. Temporary suspension — your account is paused for a period of time.
  4. Permanent ban — your account is removed for good.

Serious violations may result in immediate removal or a permanent ban.

Reporting

See something that breaks these guidelines? Use the Report button on the note or comment in the app. Reports are confidential.

Appeals

If you believe we got a moderation decision wrong, email support@nottyped.com and we'll take another look.

How we moderate

Moderation on NotTyped combines automated keyword screening with human review. Automated screening helps surface potentially harmful content, and people make the final call — because context matters, especially with handwriting.

Contact

Questions about these guidelines? Reach us at support@nottyped.com.
