Common questions

How do I write a note?

Tap the compose button (the pencil) and write one chunk at a time on the canvas — a word, a character, a number, whatever you like — using your finger, an Apple Pencil, or any stylus. Tap the checkmark to confirm each chunk; NotTyped handles the spacing and line wrapping. When you're done, post it.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account Actions → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, and all of your data is deleted within 30 days.

How do I report content?

Tap the Report button on any note or comment and choose a reason. Reports are confidential and reviewed by our moderation team.

How do I block someone?

Open the menu on a user's profile or one of their notes and choose Block. You can review and manage blocked accounts under Settings → Safety → Blocked users.

How do I make my account private?

Turn on Settings → Privacy → Private account. When your account is private, only approved followers can see your notes.

Still need help?
